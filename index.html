Computer Engineering

What Is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering can be different subjects such as hardware, software, and electrical engineering. Hardware engineering is the development of technology such as a computer which can be a desktop or server. Hardware can even be the TV you watch at night or a phone. Software engineering is the devlopment of apps or programs such as Zoom or Youtube. Electrical is the circuitry between pieces of hardware.

Education Requirements and What Computer Engineers Need To Learn

In order to become a Computer Engineer you need a bachelor's degree in computer science for an entry-level position. Computer Engineers also typically need an understanding of data structures, algorithms, numerical methods, graphics, statistics, and data visualization. Hardware engineering needs an understanding of electrical engineering and computer science.

Jobs Available In Computer Engineering

There are many job opportunities as a computer engineer. You can work as a hardware engineer building circuit boards, hard drives, and micro processors. Software engineers build websites, phone apps, or data analysis.

What Products Are Produced By Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering can produce items such as computers, laptops, TVs, or phones. Other items can be various computer parts such as computer chips, circuit boards, computer systems, and other related equipment like keyboards and mice. These smaller parts can be used to build larger systems such as robotics for manufacturing.

How We Use Computer Engineering In Every Day Life

We use computer engineering in every day life with chromebooks at school, phones, and TV. Computer engineering is more important than ever because of COVID-19 as we use it daily with Zoom and our browser. We even use it for entertainment like watching TV and playing video games.
