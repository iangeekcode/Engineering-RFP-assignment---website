What Is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering is made up of two different topics. The first of these topics is software engineering. Software engineering is the devlopment of apps or programs such as Zoom or Youtube. The second topic is hardware engineering which is the devlopment of technology such as a computer which can be a desktop or server. Hardware can even be the TV you watch at night or a phone.

Computer Engineers typical need an understanding of data structures, algorithms, numerical methods, graphics, statistics, and data visualization.
